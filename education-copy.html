Accredited Courses

  • Managing Managed Care in the Skilled Nursing Facility Environment

    Recommended for Administrators, Admissions Directors, Social Service Directors, and Case Managers.

    Medicare Advantage and other managed care payors such as Managed Medicaid, Commercial insurance and Worker’s Compensation make managing a patient SNF stay difficult.

    • * Understand and identify all parts of case managing managed care patients in SNF environment
    • * Understand best practice surrounding admissions of managed care patients in SNF environment
    • * Understand clinical updates, discharge planning and finalizing/closing the case in SNF environment
    Choose this course

  • Managing Payors in the Skilled Nursing Facility Environment

    This course is recommended for Administrators, Admissions Directors, Business Office Managers

    The challenge of keeping up with who the correct payor is month to month and at open enrollment can be daunting and mistakes can be very costly.

    With this course you will learn how to:

    • * Understand how open enrollment periods for different lines of business affect payor changes
    • * Understand how to avoid problems with payor changes monthly and at/after open enrollment periods
    • * Understand the 100 day SNF Benefit Period at payor change
    Choose this course

Non-Accredited Courses

  • What is The Role of The Admissions Director

    This course is recommended for Administrators, Admissions Directors

    Interested in pursuing a career as an Admissions Director in a Skilled Nursing Facility? This is an extremely rewarding and challenging position. This course will give you a detailed description of what it’s really like to be an Admissions Director.

    You will learn:

    • * What the major roles of an AD are
    • * What are the day-to-day responsibilities
    • * How to manage working with your team
    Choose this course

  • Introduction to Medicare and Managed Care

    This course is recommended for Administrators, Admissions Directors, Business Office Managers

    This course is an introduction to how Medicare Advantage and other managed care payors reimburse for a skilled nursing facility short-term rehabilitation stay.

    In this course, you will Understand how Medicare and Medicare Advantage Plans pay a SNF for a short term rehabilitation stay

    • * Understand Key Definitions
    • * Understand Managed Care Medicare and how it’s different
    • * Learn who pays for Long Term Care (LTC)
    Choose this course

  • Communication, Leadership and Professionalism

    This course is recommended for Administrators and Admissions Directors

    This course, geared toward Admission Directors is the perfect first course for any aspiring Admissions Director- get started on the right foot! In this course you will:

    • * Understand the importance of exemplary communication skills as an AD
    • * Understand how the AD acts as a leader in the SNF environment
    • * Define the characteristics of and embrace professionalism
    Choose this course

  • Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act

    This course is recommended for Administrators, Admissions Directors, Directors of Nursing, Social Services Directors, Therapy Directors, MDS Staff

    As an Admissions Director, you will be handling documents that contain HIPPA protected information. This course will guide you through managing this information properly and understanding the importance of the HIPPA guidelines. Upon completion of this course you will:

    • * Identify what Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPPA) is
    • * Understand how HIPPA violations may occur in a SNF AD environment
    • * Understand HIPPA penalties. Know how to help prevent HIPPA violations
    Choose this course

  • CMS Guidelines that Govern SNF Admissions

    This course is recommended for Administrators, Admissions Directors, Social Services Directors

    This course is an overview of how CMS is involved in SNF admissions and the guidelines that must be followed. In this course you will:

    • * Understand who CMS (Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services) is and how CMS governs SNF admissions
    • * Understand why CMS is involved
    • * Understand how this affects the AD
    Choose this course
